## Overall Summary
The conversation and linked sources collectively describe a major industry shift at GDC 2026: AI has moved from experimental to default infrastructure across production, UA, LiveOps, rendering, animation, and security. Tencent’s MagicDawn, VISVISE, and ACE exemplify AI‑native toolchains spanning rendering, animation, and real‑time decision systems.
