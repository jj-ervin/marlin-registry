## Overall Summary
Across all sources, the dominant theme is a global shift from fragmented integration and basic connectivity toward governed, intelligent orchestration layers. These layers unify hybrid infrastructures, coordinate automation, operationalize AI, reduce technical debt, and provide reliability, auditability, and compliance. The articles collectively emphasize modularity, agentic pipelines, workflow governance, and scalable orchestration as foundational to modern digital ecosystems. Anti‑patterns include patchwork integrations, scattered AI pilots, ungoverned models, and monolithic or channel‑centric approaches.
