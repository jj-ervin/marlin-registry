## Overall Summary
WordPress.com has expanded AI editorial capabilities through the Model Context Protocol, enabling AI agents (ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor) to draft, edit, publish, delete, and manage content across posts, pages, comments, categories, tags, and media. All actions require explicit human approval. Safety mechanisms include draft‑first behavior, warnings for live edits, recoverable deletions, role‑based permissions, and full activity logging. The articles collectively emphasize workflow automation, authenticated connectors, oversight, regulatory implications, and the potential for AI to scale small operators to enterprise‑level capability.
