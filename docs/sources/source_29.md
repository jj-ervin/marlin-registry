## Overall Summary
The conversation and all linked sources converge on a shared theme: AI transformation is constrained not by technology but by culture, trust, adaptability, and organizational readiness. Deloitte’s Human Capital Trends report introduces cultural debt, change overload, and the need for identity-led leadership. Forbes emphasizes trust frameworks and agentic AI, positioning trust as the foundation for value creation. Libertify expands on adaptability gaps, culture inhibitors, and human–AI work design. Additional Deloitte sources reinforce the need for human-centric AI strategies and capability development. Together, these sources highlight the structural, cultural, and leadership prerequisites for sustainable AI transformation.
